About Me

Hello, I'm Sonny George. I've just finishing my Master's at Brandeis University and am seeking research and other opportunities in the space of action-taking AI or software development generally.

Much of future impactful AI will require some form of active embodiment, reflection, and continual learning. In contrast, the “intelligence” of today's AI systems tends to be static and task-specific.

I am especially excited by the development of AI systems that:

  • Plan and act in uncertain environments
  • Adapt to novel tasks/situations with inference-time-feasible efficiency and without catastrophic forgetting
  • Are sufficiently interpretable and manipulable to be aligned with human preferences
